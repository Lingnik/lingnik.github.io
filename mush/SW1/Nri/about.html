Alliance Intelligence is the nervous system of the New Republic. Through its activities, the government is kept informed of the activities and intentions of the Empire, various criminal organizations, and even the New Republic's allies and citizens. The Intelligence branch has moved out from under the direct control of the military, as it had been during the days of the Rebellion, and answers directly to the Inner Council. Intelligence operations not only keep the military abreast of Imperial actions; they also serve as the prime information-gathering organ for the civil government.

Ranging from the data sifter to the field operative, an agent plays out many roles during their career. Every new recruit is brought into Intel as an operative in the Internal Section. This section deals with the training of new recruits, data-sifting, and administrative functions of the branch. Passive operatives are low profile intelligence agents that specialize in disguise, deception and data collection. The are trained to fit in to any situation and are most often very unremarkable in appearance, regardless of race or species. Active intelligence is the branch that deals with high-risk missions. Abductions, rescues, physical theft of information or property and even the occasional assassination are possible mission types. This branch takes its orders from the Inner Council only and most missions are never heard about, regardless of outcome.
